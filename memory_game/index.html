Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match,. Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-sujaki, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn overpairs of matching cards.
